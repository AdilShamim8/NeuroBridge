Q4 revenue was $12.8M (about $1.9M higher than Q3).

Gross margin rose from 41.2% to 44.7%.
That is an increase of 3.5 percentage points.

Operating expense increased 8.5%.
Think of this as about $8.50 more cost per $100.

Net income was $1.6M.
That equals a 12.5% net margin (about $12.50 kept per $100 revenue).

Cash reserves ended at $6.3M after $0.9M in capital spending.
